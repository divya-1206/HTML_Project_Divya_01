Naruto

TV Series 2002-2007

Naruto is a Japanese manga serieswritten and illustrated by Masashi Kishimoto. It tells the story of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja who seeks recognition from his peers and dreams of becoming the Hokage, the leader of his village.The story is told in two parts —the first set in Naruto's pre-teen years, and the second in his teens. The series is based on two one-shot manga by Kishimoto: Karakuri (1995), which earned Kishimoto an honorable mention in Shueisha's monthly Hop Step Award the following year, and Naruto (1997).

Naruto characters are some of the most recognizable and beloved in the anime community.While there are a lot of characters to choose from,these 10 are by far some of the most memorable and unforgettable in the franchise.Each is incredibly powerful in their own unique ways, and they have all had an impressive impact on the series as a whole.

List Of Best Characters In Naruto

  1. Hitachi Uchiha

  2. Kakashi Hatake

  3. Minato Namikaze

  4. Naruto Uzumaki

  5. Jiraiya

  6. Shikamaru Nara

  7. Nine-tailed Demon Fox

  8. Obito Uchiha

  9. Might Guy

  10. Hinata Hyuga

1.Hitachi Uchiha

Having massacred his own clan, leaving only his brother alive, Itachi went on to join the Akatsuki.While upfront this seems horrible, everything he did he did to safeguard his village.Unfortunately for his brother, his good intentions and his cooperation with the Third Hokage weren’t revealed until after his death.Itachi’s character development was some of the best in the Naruto anime and fans couldn’t help but feel bad for the position he was put in, as it was one of the most heartbreaking in the series.As a ninja, Itachi was a genius who showed skill beyond his years. He rose rapidly through the ranks, rivaling even Shisui, who at the time was regarded as the strongest Uchiha.

2.Kakashi Hitake

Famed as the Kakashi of the Sharingan, Kakashi Hatake is one of the most talented individuals in the ninja world, and he eventually became the Sixth Hokage. While he is undeniably powerful, his skills are so much more than they appear on the surface.A master of lighting release, his purple electricity can be devastating, and he has learned over one thousand Jutsu.Even Itachi couldn’t outflank him, and he was able to force Kakuzu and Kisame into a deadlock. Without him and his leadership, Kaguya wouldn’t have been defeated as it was his Kamui Lighting Cutter that injured her.

3.Minato Namikaze

He excelled in all five basic nature transformations, could summon toads like Jiraiya, and his Flying Thunder God Technique allowed him to teleport himself and anyone or anything — including projectiles and tailed beasts — to wherever he wished.Minato was much more than just Naruto’s father, he was an extremely powerful and well-rounded character in his own right, which makes him one of the most beloved characters in the Naruto anime.

4.Naruto Uzumaki

Of course, we couldn’t leave out the title character, and the Naruto anime wouldn’t exist without its main character.So far,he is the strongest shinobi in the entire Naruto series and he’s known as the Hero of the Hidden Leaf Village.Through the series, he has faced a great deal of hardship that has challenged his abilities as a ninja, but he has prevailed thus far, even achieving his dream of becoming the Seventh Hokage.

5.Jiraiya

One of Konohagahure’s most legendary shinobi, Jiraiya was hailed as the strongest ninja of his generation and through his travels, he wrote down all of his learned knowledge so that it could be passed on to his godson Naturo.If he was female, he’d definitely be regarded as one of the best white-haired anime girls, and his impressive hair isn’t just for show, as he can grow and manipulate his locks to bind and destroy targets.

6.Shikamaru Nara

A shinobi from the Nara clan, Shikamaru appears to be incredibly lazy, but he actually possesses an extraordinary intellect that allows him to prevail easily in combat. As a member of the Nara clan, he is able to manipulate his own shadow, and his signature move is Shadow Imitation, which he learned even before attending the Academy. In the Naruto anime, he is also shown to be proficient in fuinjutsu and able to create clones of himself by using his chakra to shape his shadow or by using the Shadow Clone Technique.

7.Nine-tailed Demon Fox

The Nine-Tails emerged during the last days of Hagoromo Otsutsuki‘s life when the Sage split the chakra of the Ten-Tailed Beast into monstrous constructs of pure chakra, entities that would later be known as the tailed beasts. The Nine-Tailed Demon Beast is as sadistic as he is cunning, and he has a deep hatred for the Uchiha clan.

8.Obito Uchiha

A member of the Uchiha clan, he was thought to have died in the Third Shinobi War only to be discovered to have been saved and trained by Madara.Under the name Tobi, he took control of the Akatsuki as a means to try and start the Fourth Shinobi War but ended up having a change of heart, sacrificing himself to save the world as it is, instead of trying to replace it.

9.Might Guy

Might Guy, the hilarious character with the thickest eyebrows, is a deceptively powerful character known for his Strong Fist style.While he never mastered ninjutsu, he is a master of taijutsu, and without any chakra enhancement, his physical prowess is absolutely astounding, giving him the ability to pull off seemingly superhuman feats.

10.Hinata Hyuga

After being defended from bullies by Naruto Uzumaki, Hinata was inspired by the boy and wanted to emulate him.While her kind and soft-spoken personality originally hindered her abilities, she was able to grow stronger after resolving to be more like Naruto.With him as her main source of motivation, she was able to grow to be incredibly strong and brave, even facing Pain knowing she had no way to win.
